About this channel

Unnamed Things examines real criminal cases with the intention of raising awareness, understanding investigative processes, and highlighting systemic issues. The channel is educational and focused on prevention and context — never to sensationalize or glorify violent acts.

Mission: I create videos to raise awareness and educate about traumatic crimes — never to sensationalize or glorify violence.

How topics are handled

  • Research is based on public records and verified reporting; sources are cited where available.
  • Victims and families are treated with respect; identifying details that could cause harm are omitted.
  • No graphic images or gratuitous detail — content focuses on facts, analysis, and lessons for prevention.
  • Disclaimers and resources (hotlines, support organisations) will be provided where appropriate.
