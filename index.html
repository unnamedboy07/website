About this channel

UnnamedThings explores real criminal cases with the goal of increasing public awareness, understanding criminal investigation techniques, and respectfully examining systemic issues that contribute to violent crime. The purpose of this channel is educational and preventative — not to sensationalize or glorify violence.

Rewritten mission statement: I create videos to raise awareness and educate about traumatic crimes — never to sensationalize or glorify violence.

How topics are handled

  • Cases are researched with public sources, and covered with sensitivity to victims and families.
  • No explicit or graphic imagery is used. Content focuses on facts, context, and prevention.
  • Sources and disclaimers will be listed in each video description when available.
